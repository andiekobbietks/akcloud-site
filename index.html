What Are Cloud Credits

Free infrastructure funding the hyperscalers hand to startups. Most founders never claim it.

Cloud credits are grants of compute, storage and AI capacity, often worth one thousand to one hundred and fifty thousand dollars or more, that Microsoft, AWS and Google give to early stage companies. They let you run servers, databases and GPU workloads for free instead of burning your runway on infrastructure. AKCloud gets you qualified, approved at the highest tier, and builds production systems that run on those credits.
